To verify the operator is running:

  kubectl get pods -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l "app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "identity-claim-operator.name" . }},app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}"

To create an IdentityClaim:

  cat <<EOF | kubectl apply -f -
  apiVersion: identity.cluster.local/v1alpha1
  kind: IdentityClaim
  metadata:
    name: example-claim
    namespace: default
  spec:
    selector:
      matchLabels:
        app: my-app
    ttl: "1h"
  EOF

To check the status of your claims:

  kubectl get identityclaims -A
